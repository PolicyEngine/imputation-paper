\section{Conclusion}
\label{sec:conclusion}

Imputation sits upstream of every population statistic a microsimulation
model produces, and the files it produces are reweighted by consumers the
imputer never sees. We presented an estimator that treats survey weights as a
structural obligation rather than an option --- weighted bootstrap into the
forests, directly-weighted regime gates, sequential chaining, and an
interface that refuses a silent unweighted fit --- and evaluated it against
standard survey-imputation methods with ablations that attribute performance
