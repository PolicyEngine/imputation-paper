\section{Data and tasks}
\label{sec:data}

Four tasks span the settings the estimator is used in. Each defines a donor
table, a receiver table, predictors shared by both, and target variables
observed only in the donor; the protocol of Section~\ref{sec:protocol} applies
identically to all.

\subsection{Task 1: wealth from the SCF onto the CPS}

The Survey of Consumer Finances \citep{fed2023scf} observes household net
worth; the \cps{} Annual Social and Economic Supplement \citep{census2024}
does not. We impute net worth from the \scf{} onto \cps{} households from
shared demographic and income predictors, the task the \microimpute{}
manuscript introduced. The policy application is the Supplemental Security
Income asset test: \textsc{SSI} eligibility depends on countable resources
\citep{ssa2024ssi}, so simulating the baseline caseload --- and reforms such
as the SSI Savings Penalty Elimination Act \citep{ssi_spea_2025}, which would
raise the resource limits --- requires imputed wealth. \todo{State the

\subsection{Task 2: cross-survey structure on CPS, SIPP, and PSID}

Each of the \cps{} \citep{census2024}, \sipp{} \citep{census2023sipp}, and
\psid{} \citep{psid2023} observes variables the others lack. We impute each
survey's non-shared variables from shared demographics and score joint
structure against that survey's own held-out records with \prdc{} coverage
\citep{naeem2020reliable}. This is the task where chaining should matter most:
unchained imputation preserves marginals while attenuating the dependence the
holdout exhibits. \todo{State the variable surfaces per survey from the frozen

\subsection{Task 3: zero-inflated tax components}

Dividend and interest income in tax microdata are zero for most records,
positive with a heavy tail for the rest, and central to revenue estimation.
We impute \puf{}-style income components under the protocol, score zero-share
preservation and tail fit, and run the reweighting stress test of
Section~\ref{sec:metrics}: prior \policyengine{} production experience
motivates this task, where an unweighted fit broadcast rare high-value donor
records across a receiver file as near-zero-weight point masses that later
reweighting amplified. All fragility numbers reported here are measured under
this paper's protocol. \todo{Task config, component list, and donor/receiver
